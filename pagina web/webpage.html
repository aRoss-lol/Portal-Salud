Welcome to Portal Salud

Your reliable source of information on public health in Uruguay.

At Portal Health, we are committed to providing you with clear and accessible information about public health in our country. We know that understanding how the health system works is essential for making informed decisions about your well-being and that of your family.

Here you will find essential resources on health topics, as well as a comprehensive guide to the various health centers available in Uruguay. From hospitals and clinics to primary care centers, we help you locate the service that best suits your needs.

Our goal is to empower you with the knowledge necessary to take care of your health and that of your loved ones. Browse through our sections and discover how we can support you on your journey to a healthier life.
