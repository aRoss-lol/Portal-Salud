Regular Checkups with Your Primary Care Physician

Schedule annual check-ups to monitor your overall health, get blood tests, and receive recommended vaccinations. Maintaining an ongoing relationship with your doctor will help detect health issues early.

Access to Preventive Care

Don't wait until you're sick to visit a health center. Take advantage of public health campaigns and prevention programs, such as screenings for chronic diseases, often offered at clinics and hospitals.

Immediate Care for Emergencies

Familiarize yourself with the nearest emergency care centers to your home. In a critical situation, knowing where to go can make a difference in the care you receive.

Participate in Health Workshops and Talks

Many health centers organize educational activities on nutrition, stress management, and disease prevention. Attending these events will provide you with valuable information and connect you with health professionals.
