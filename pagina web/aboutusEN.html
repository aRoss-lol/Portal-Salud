About Portal Salud

At Portal Salud, our mission is to provide basic public health information in an accessible, attractive, and artistic way. We understand that access to clear and reliable information is essential to empower individuals and contribute to the well-being of our community.

We strive to avoid misinformation by offering useful and updated resources that address relevant health topics for all Uruguayans. We believe that an informed population is a healthier population.

For any inquiries or suggestions, please feel free to contact us via email at: portalsaluduy@gmail.com. We are here to help and support you on your journey to better health.
